Instruction Stack
PUSH_INT [arg] -> Pushes the parameter to the top of the stack, where the parameter is an integer literal
ADD -> Pop the top two items from the stack, add them, and push result back onto the stack (a+b == b+a)
SUB -> Pop the top two items from the stack, subtract them, and push result back onto the stack (a-b)
MUL -> Pop the top two items from the stack, multiply them, and push result back onto the stack (a+b == b+a)
DIV -> Pop the top two items from the stack, divide them, and push result back onto the stack (a/b)
